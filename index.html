London borough ranked

There are many bad things reported about London on the media right now. Whether if it is the epidemic of knife crime or the sky high renting prices

Hackney, Islington, Camden and Southwark might have bad names associated with them, but is that really the case? What do the local residents say about the area? It is an experiment and a little guide for people who is moving to London soon to find their perfect place.

This website collected data from various sites and scraped twitter to see how each borough is doing in terms of rent, crime rate, leisure and what are people saying about each borough.

The data might surprise you! Try it out and find your perfect borough!

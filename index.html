Where should you live in London?

By Naomi Guo

There are many bad things about London in the media right now. Whether if it is the epidemic of knife crime or the sky-high property prices.

Some boroughs such as Hackney and Camden might have bad names associated with high crime rate, but is that the case? What do the residents say about the area?

This website collected data from various official sites and scraped twitter to see what's going on in the neighbourhood.

It is an experiment and a little guide for people who are moving to London soon or people who is thinking of moving to find their perfect place.
